## Team Culture and Values

### Open Source First
The AIPG team is committed to transparency and open-source development. All code, documentation, and technical decisions are made in the open, allowing the community to participate, audit, and contribute.

### Fair Launch Philosophy
No team pre-mines, no VC allocations, no insider advantages. The team earns alongside the community through meaningful contributions to the network.

### Global and Distributed
With team members across multiple continents and time zones, AIPG operates as a truly global, distributed organization. This diversity brings varied perspectives and ensures 24/7 development and support.

### Community-Driven
The team actively engages with the AIPG community through Discord, Telegram, and social media. User feedback directly influences development priorities and feature roadmaps.

### Innovation Focus
From AI model integration to blockchain infrastructure, the team constantly pushes boundaries to deliver cutting-edge technology that democratizes AI access.
